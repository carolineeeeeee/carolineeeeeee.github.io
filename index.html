Research Interests

    I am broadly interested in the intersection of SE and AI:
  • Applying SE methodologies to AI (SE4AI) to build safe, robust, and secure systems;
  • Leveraging AI to advance SE (AI4SE) to improve the security and maintainability of software.

Keywords: Human-Centric Reliability, Trustworthy AI and AI Testing and Verification.
